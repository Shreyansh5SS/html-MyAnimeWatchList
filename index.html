Hi there everyone reading my script here. Welcome to my first frontend creation, it is about a genre of entertainment which i admire a lot, watch a lot. Anime or Japanese Manga animation adaptations are well known to add impact to the culture around the world including myself.
Hence I wanted to showcase some of the good and bad animes that I've watched so far.

Favourite Nine

Attack on Titan

This is one of the animes I've watched in the early days of my anime journey. I still believe that there isn't a better story or plot in any other story in the world.

Demon Slayer

People say that it is carried by its animation qualtity alone, cuz it has a simple storyline. In my opinion, it carries all the emotions that exist in the world.

One Piece

It is an anime that has been airing for decades now and the story has still not ended. Undoubtedly, one piece has the deepest world building of any anime.

Your lie in April

There is just one thing that I can say about this anime, it likes to rip your heart apart slowly like a poision. It was the anime that broke me.

Rent a Girlfriend

One of the shittiest animes to ever exist, the main protagonist doen't seem to enjoy a character development. I watch it just for Chizuru Mitzuhara.

Bunnygirl Senpai

It is an existing proof of the phrase, "Don't judge a book by its cover". Thought of it as another Rom-com, it actually was just a journey of emotions throughout.

Classroom of the Elite

It does not have super powers, just strong willed and ultra intellect masterminds fighting to be the best. And our aura farmer Ayanokoji, the man just can't lose.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Nothing much to say apart from the fact that Satorou Gojo is the strongest man to ever exist. Brutal series, doesn't hesitate to kill your favourite character.

Solo Levelling

It pretty much defines its name, Jinwhoo Sung, a man who single handedly gets to the top of the top, continuously farming aura along the way.
